History

April 17, 2024

Farfetch is an online store for luxury fashion. In mid-2021, it worked with 550 brands and 2,500 stores in 49 countries. It had two million regular customers and 10 million visits to its website every month. Farfetch helps fashion brands and small stores sell their products online worldwide, even in different countries like Angola and Zimbabwe. They keep their small physical stores but can reach a global market. The website takes an average fee of 28 percent for each sale, which is lower than what is usually charged in the fashion industry.

Many of Farfetch’s customers are young adults. The company also says that the rest of its customers think like young adults, meaning they are good with technology and like shopping online.

José Neves started Farfetch in 2008. He is a business leader from Portugal but works in Britain. The company’s main office is in London, but it also has branches in big cities like New York and Shanghai. Most of its growth in sales comes from China, the Middle East, Latin America, and Eastern Europe. In 2020, Farfetch started selling luxury goods on two of Alibaba’s online platforms in China.

Farfetch has shown that luxury shoppers don’t always need to see and touch products before buying them online. They also sell luxury items at discounts, which is unusual because luxury brands usually don’t offer sales online. This discounting has caused some issues with other stores lowering their prices too.

In 2018, despite not making a profit yet and having lost $255 million since 2015, Farfetch decided to sell its shares to the public. The company was valued at $5 billion, making it one of the most valuable fashion companies. This high value was surprising because other online stores like Net-a-Porter sell similar items at similar prices. However, the people managing the sale of shares believed Farfetch could grow quickly, similar to how Amazon grew before it started making profits.

Farfetch has also put a lot of effort into using data and improving customer service, much like Amazon. In 2020, they had 1,300 employees working on technology and data, making up 40 percent of their workforce.
